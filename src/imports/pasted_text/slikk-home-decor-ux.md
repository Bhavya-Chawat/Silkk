Design a high-fidelity mobile ecommerce experience for Slikk, a Bangalore-based quick commerce platform that delivers fashion and lifestyle products within 60 minutes.

Slikk is launching a new category called “Home Decor” targeting urban Gen Z and young millennials aged 18-30 living in Bangalore, especially HSR Layout, Koramangala, and Indiranagar.

The app experience should feel like:
Pinterest + Nykaa + Instagram shopping + quick commerce

IMPORTANT:
This should NOT look like Amazon or Flipkart.
The experience should feel:
- aesthetic-first
- trend-first
- editorial
- social-commerce inspired
- highly visual
- emotionally driven
- modern Gen Z lifestyle focused

PRIMARY USER:
Young renters, creators, working professionals, couples, and students who want to make their rooms look aesthetic on a budget.

PRICE RANGE:
Most products should visually communicate affordability between ₹499–₹799.

CORE BRAND THEMES:
- “Room glow-up in 60 mins”
- “Pinterest-inspired homes”
- “Affordable aesthetics”
- “Try & Buy”
- “Impulse decor shopping”
- “Soft girl aesthetic”
- “Cozy room vibes”
- “WFH desk setup”
- “Host-ready homes”

VISUAL STYLE:
- warm modern tones
- soft beige backgrounds
- editorial layouts
- rounded cards
- premium but playful typography
- clean spacing
- Gen Z visual merchandising
- subtle gradients
- modern minimalism
- stylish room imagery
- high quality lifestyle photography

DO NOT:
- make it cluttered
- use marketplace-style dense grids
- use enterprise dashboards
- make it look generic
- overload with categories
